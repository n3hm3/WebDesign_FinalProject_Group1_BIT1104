Our History and Affiliation

The Sierra Leone Football Association is a proud member of the West African Football Union (WAFU), Confederation of African Football (CAF), and the International Federation of Football Associations (FIFA).

Established in 1960, we have been the nation's only football governing body, dedicated to managing and advancing the beautiful game across Sierra Leone.
